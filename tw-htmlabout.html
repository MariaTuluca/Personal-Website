About me:

Hi! My name is Tuluca Maria-Laura and I was born on April 18 2005. I am a second year student at University Politehnica Bucharest, Faculty of Automatic Control and Computer Science. I enjoy reading, spending time with my family and exploring new cultures through travel.

These are my two siblings! My sister's name is Daria-Nicoleta, she was born in August 2009 and she's a freshman in highschool. My brother's name is Matei-Ioan, he was born in November 2021 and he just started kindergarten.

Between 2011 and 2024, I was part of the artistic group "Magic Star" for modern and hip-hop dances and the artistic group "Grailul Armanesc" for Aromanian dances. We participated in national and international competitions, where we received many awards.

For 3 years, I practiced kickboxing and Kali, a Filipino martial art involving weapons, at a club in Calarasi. During this time, I pushed both my physical and mental boundaries, all while enjoying the process. I also earned my green belt in Kali Arnis Eskrima in May 2024.

I also love to travel! Sometimes, my life seems to be time-starved and technology-driven. Travel allows me to escape life’s daily demands, dramas and deadlines and it enables me to clear my mind and charge my batteries.
